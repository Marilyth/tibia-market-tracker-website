Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14th of February 2025

Tibia Market Tracker ("the Application" and "the Bot") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data while using the Application and the Discord bot ("the Bot").

1. Data Collection

The Application does not collect, store, or process any personal information. No user data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

The Bot stores user settings on our server, specifically the user ID paired with their selected Tibia world. This data is used solely to provide personalized bot responses.

2. Local Storage Usage

The Application utilizes local storage to save user-defined filters and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not shared externally.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application and the Bot do not integrate with third-party services that collect or track user data.

4. Security

The stored user settings are only used within the Bot and are not shared externally.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be reflected in the Application and the Bot with an updated effective date.

6. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us in the support server.
